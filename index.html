About Me

I am a PhD student at School of Information Systems, Singapore Management University currently working with Associate Professor David Lo in the SOAR group. Before joining PhD at SMU I had worked as a Software Developer for Accenture as well as TCS Ltd., developing and maintaining enterprise software for Insurance and Airline industry clients. You can have a look at my LinkedIn profile for further details.I am a native of a small Himalayan state called Himachal Pradesh in India and have been born and brought up there till my graduation from National Institute of Technology, Hamirpur in 2008. I like to follow football and gadgets.
